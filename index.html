About Me

UI & UX Designer visualizing and crafting intuitive solutions that meet user needs and goals. With over 12 years of experience I champion the process of design thinking; creating innovative product experience.

Strong eye for clean, pixel perfect UI design and a relentless attention to detail, strong sense of design theory and typography, discovery and framing, sketching, wireframing, icon designing, prototyping, storytelling, solving key design problems through ideation, designer led user research, measuring and rebuilding and providing development support are all central to my creative approach & process, but what I enjoy most is collaborating with fellow designers and other teams solving design challenges.

I have a passion for user experience design, with focus on building functional and usable products. Having worked on projects for leading marques like AAA Insurance, Fisher-Price, Toro, Disney, Thrivent Financials for Lutherans, USAA and more one thing I always evangelized; “If the user doesn’t get it it’s not worth it… go back to the drawing board”.

Digital Strategy

The Challenge

Insurance Portal is a web app that allows insurance agents to service policyholders with coverage details information, documents, online payments etc. Besides UI uplift this was a complete overhaul of the user experience with better brand identity. Right from personas to validating information architecture the need was for a product that would be user friendly, inviting, clean, and functional.

The Solution

Responsive design to provide seamless experience to agents as they work between desktop and mobile devices. Scalable information architecture and navigation to accomodate current and future functionalities. Overall brand alignment that embodies the visual direction for a suite of products that experientially celebrates the customer-agent relationship.

Lead Designer

UX Design

As a UX specialist, I partnered with various product analysts to work through the early stages of a design or product working from strategy and feature definition into information architecture, sketching and prototyping, visual design, testing and iterating.

User Research

Participated in user research and synthesis through designer lead user research (DLUR) and contextual enquiry. Created click-throughs and prototypes for web and mobile/tablet for use during tests. Synthesized and compiled findings to inform UX strategy

Collaboration

Established collaboration and communication between the design, development, and business leadership teams. I implemented formal design reviews, created best practices around documentation (lean UX) and specification

The Problem

Dingo is a web app that allows pasive job seekers to connect with recruiters and visa versa. They came to me with a great idea in need of an identity and design. They needed a brand that would be friendly, inviting, clean, and functional: as well as a story that they could infuse throughout their marketing content.

The Solution

I worked closely with the Dingo team to create a cohesive brand identity and streamlined user experience that would highlight the ease and functionality of the web-app. Responsive card based dashboard design to provide seamless experience to the users across various devices.

Product Designer

Strategy & Research

My process started with research; unveiling the real problem that we need to solve and getting more of a sense of what user needs and wants by facilitating user interviews and usability testing and creating personas.

Brand Identity

Before creating the landing page, I put in a lot of effort into the content that will be available on the website. That process involved creating the website content, copy, color, Dingo branding, typography, style guide, logo and photography.

UX Design

On the go or in the office, I designed Dingo to work for passive job seekers lifestyle. Search, connect, and track on any device; it's that easy. And with realtime notifications, the users never miss a thing.
